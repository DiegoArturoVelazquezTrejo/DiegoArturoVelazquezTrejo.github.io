Biological Data Analytics & AI Consulting

Consulting for biological and clinical datasets—including longitudinal cohorts, cross-sectional studies, epidemiology, and multi-omics (transcriptomics, proteomics, metabolomics)—with additional focus on pharmaceutical and biotech analytics (clinical development, biomarker strategy, patient stratification, real-world evidence, and translational modelling). Analytic Outcomes

Minimal, decision-oriented outputs—always with documented assumptions and uncertainty.

Descriptive & Data Quality

  • Profiling + QC summaries
  • Batch/drift checks
  • Missingness + bias audit
  • Analysis-ready tables

Inference & Evidence

  • Adjusted associations
  • Effect sizes + CI/posterior
  • Robustness / sensitivity checks
  • Clear limitations

Prediction, Risk & Forecasts

  • Risk scores / classifiers
  • Calibration + thresholds
  • Survival risk (time-to-event)
  • Forecasts with uncertainty

Machine Learning (Classic)

  • Classification: Logistic Reg., SVM, Random Forest, XGBoost
  • Regression: Ridge/Lasso/ElasticNet, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting
  • Clustering: k-means, Hierarchical, Gaussian Mixtures
  • Dimensionality reduction: PCA, UMAP, t-SNE
  • Feature selection: L1/Lasso, Boruta, permutation importance
  • Time-to-event: CoxPH, survival forests, boosted survival

Computational Chemistry (Docking & Dynamics)

  • Virtual screening: filtering + ranking of candidate ligands
  • Molecular docking: poses, interaction checks, consensus rescoring
  • Molecular dynamics: stability of protein–ligand complex over time
  • Key metrics: RMSD/RMSF, H-bond occupancy, contact stability, key distances
  • Deliverable: prioritized shortlist + interpretable evidence (figures & rationale)

Pharma & Biotech (Decision Support)

  • Biomarker programs: discovery → validation, feature robustness, confounder control.
  • Patient stratification: responder/non-responder signatures, subgroup stability, interpretability.
  • Clinical endpoints: risk models, calibration, threshold setting, sensitivity/specificity tradeoffs.
  • Translational evidence: pathway context, network analysis, mechanism-oriented interpretation.
  • Reproducibility: auditable code + data dictionaries + methodology appendix for review.

Discovery & Integration

  • Stratification / clustering
  • Biomarker signatures (biomarker discovery)
  • Multi-omics integration
  • Networks (coexpression, GRNs)

Interpretation & Discussion

Discussion

  • Next experimental/clinical steps
